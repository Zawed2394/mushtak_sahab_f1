  • Spouse: Ayesha Khatoon
  • Children: Ramla Mushtaque, Shaker Mushtaque, Yasir Mushtaque, Sharique Mushtaque
  • Education:
    • Alimiyat: Darul Uloom Nadwatul Ulama, Lucknow
    • Fazilat: Jamia Islamia Faiz-e-Aam, Maunath Bhanjan, Uttar Pradesh
    • M.A. Urdu: Maulana Azad National Urdu University, Hyderabad
    • B.Ed.
  • Occupation: Teacher at Higher Secondary School, Hafalganj, Katihar, Bihar
